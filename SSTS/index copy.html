Requirement Gathering

In the context of PP woven bags, requirement gathering serves as the foundational stage where specific needs and preferences for the bags are comprehensively identified.

  • Conduct interviews, surveys, and focus groups with customers and stakeholders.
  • Gather information on size, functionality, durability, and stylistic preferences.
  • Compile a detailed list of requirements to guide the design process.

Design

In the design phase, detailed sketches and digital prototypes are crafted based on the gathered requirements for PP woven bags.

  • Create sketches and digital prototypes based on gathered requirements.
  • Consider color schemes, materials, and innovative features.
  • Collaborate between design and engineering teams for practicality and aesthetics.
  • Maintain transparent communication channels to discuss design elements such as color schemes, materials, and innovative features, obtaining client confirmation at each stage.

Making

Involve the client in the quality assurance process, seeking confirmation that the produced bags meet their standards.

  • Select materials that align with design and durability requirements.
  • Determine manufacturing processes and set up the production line.
  • Implement rigorous quality control measures throughout the manufacturing process.

Prototyping and Final Product

  • Distribute bags to real users for feedback and identify areas for improvement.
  • Iterate on the design based on feedback before moving to mass production.
  • Launch the final product into the market with a focus on marketing and distribution.
